

What is sprint burndown chart?
Burndown chart provide a graphical view of the remaining work vs time frame left in the sprint 
to complete it.
It compares between actual work remaining vs Ideal work remaining.

X-axes shows timeline of the sprint.
Y-axes shows the remaining work in story points

This chart tells only about the sprint.
It includes effort of the main task or story.
It does not include efforts of subtask.

Can burndown chart be negative? how?
Yes, it can be negative.
When we started story and then if changed in estimation then it can be go into negative.
