Velocity is the work done by a team in a time boxed interval.

Velocity charts are comparison charts for commitment vs delivery with respect to different iteration.

Velocity chart: 
Blue bar: Commitment.
Orange Bar: Delivered.

Y axes: Measure of work
X axes: Number of sprints.



Sprints   committed  Delivered
Sprint1      120       108
Sprint2      135       110
Sprint3      130       120
Sprint4      125       125
Sprint5      130       120







Uses:
Depicts the performance of the team in past and present times.
Depicts if the team is close in their Estimations.
Guides the team in planning activities like sprint planning and release planning.
